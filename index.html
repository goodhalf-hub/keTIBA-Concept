Welcome to the Kenya Travel Industry Business Awards

About the KeTIBA Awards

The KeTIBA Awards, organized by the Kenya Association of Travel Agents (KATA), celebrate excellence in the travel and tourism industry. These prestigious awards recognize outstanding contributions by travel agencies, suppliers, and individuals who have significantly impacted the industry.

The awards have a long-standing history of honoring the best in the travel business, serving as a platform for excellence and inspiring innovation in travel services.

KATA's Role in the KeTIBA Awards

KATA (Kenya Association of Travel Agents) is at the forefront of organizing and supporting the KeTIBA Awards. Through this initiative, KATA highlights the exceptional efforts of businesses and individuals who contribute to the growth and success of Kenyaâ€™s travel industry.

As the primary organizing body, KATA ensures that the awards are conducted with the highest level of transparency and fairness, in line with industry standards.

Award Categories

Best Travel Agency Award

Recognizing travel agencies that have shown excellence in service delivery, client satisfaction, and innovation.

Lifetime Achievement Award

Honoring long-term contributions to the industry, selected by the KATA Board.

Best Travel Suppliers Award

Acknowledging suppliers who contribute to the travel and tourism industry through products and services.
